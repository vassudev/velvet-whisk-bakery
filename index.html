About Us

Crafting red velvet and artisan bakes in the heart of Trivandrum.

The Velvet Whisk opened with one goal: to bring the finest red velvet cakes and pastries to Kowdiar. Every layer is baked in-house, and every slice is a blend of tradition and a touch of the future.

We source quality ingredients locally where we can, and we’re proud to be your neighbourhood bakery—whether you’re stopping by for a coffee and a croissant or ordering a celebration cake.
